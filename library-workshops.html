Workshop Recordings

Recordings of the September 2023 workshop on CBIO led by Dr. Henry Perry at Duke University — attended on-site and online by 75 people from around the world, with presentations for CBIO implementers in Bolivia, Guatemala, and Kenya.

Each day is available in English (YouTube) and as downloadable Spanish and French video recordings. The Spanish and French files are large; on a slow connection, allow time for them to load.
